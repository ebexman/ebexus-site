## About $EBEX
$EBEX is a community-driven token created as an experimental initiative on the blockchain. Its goal is to build a stronger community together. Our mission is to support a strong and collaborative community around the Ebexus token, exploring innovative uses of blockchain technology and laying the foundation for future developments in the ecosystem.

- **Total Supply**: 25,000,000 (fixed)
- **Team Vesting**: 10% — fully community-focused
- **No hidden minting** — transparent and fair
